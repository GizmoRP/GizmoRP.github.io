Miner

Step 1

Go to "Miner's Locker Room" on your map and change to "workclothes"

Step 2

Use a company- or private car and drive to "Rock" on your map.

Step 3

Drive to "Rock Washing" on your map to clean the rocks

Step 4

Drive to "Rock Smelting" on your map to get different ores.

Step 5

Drive to "Copper Seller" on your map in order to sell your Coppers

Drive to "Iron Seller" on your map in order to sell Iron.

Drive to "Gold Seller" on your map in order to sell your Gold.

Drive to "Diamond Seller" on your map in order to sell your Diamonds.
