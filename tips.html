Tips & Tricks!

✔

 Ignorance is not an excuse! Read and follow our server Rules!

✔

 You can load up alot more items in the back of your car!

✔

 There are some pretty decent rewards when successfully robbing bank!

✔

 You can purchase up to 5 FIXKITS at Gas Stations if you want to fix your own car at dire moments.

✔

 When you die, you lose everything in your inventory. Including normal money and black money.

✔

 Remember to eat Bread and drink Water! Buy'em at stores.

✔

 You can store Blackmoney inside apartments or vehicles if you are afraid of holding them yourself.

✔

 Black money needs to be whitewashed, before it can be put to use.

✔

 We use realistic vehicles on our server! So do your best to avoid crashing!

✔

 You can get ahold of guns a good few other ways than buying them!

✔

 Join our Discord server for announcements and other important info on the server!

★

 If you die, remember to press G on your keyboard for assistance!

★

 Press Z on your keyboard to open the Scoreboard.

★

 Press L when close to a the back of your car to open the car's Trunk.

★

 Press Y while driving to activate Cruise Control.

★

 Press X on you keyboard to Surrender yourself.

★

 Press F1 on you keyboard to open your Phone.

★

 Press F2 on you keyboard to open your Inventory.

★

 Press F3 on you keyboard to perform Emotes & Animations.

★

 Press F7 on you keyboard to open your Invoices.
