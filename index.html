Who are we?

This is a Roleplaying GTA V-Private Server using the Fivem-Engine.
We struggle to keep everything as bug-free, realistic and professional as possible for you as a player.
Our group consist of a few Coders, Graphic Designers and Web Developers, doing their best to
give you and your friends a worthy experience in this roleplay-community.


why choose us?

We provide a fun and realistic playtime due to the steps we have taken to make this possible.
On our server we also offer a wast amount of things to do. Both legal and illegal jobs for you to make a name for yourself.
But let's not forget about all of our dozens different cars, bikes, boats and even aircrafts!

There are also several active Police, EMS and Mechanics on the server at all times!
Why wait? Click here to try us out!
